Briam

Description

Traditonal Greek summer dishh that is easy to make and vegan. It's made of slices of roasted vegetables baked in a Greek seasoned puree. Anything that you grow in your garden can be used in this dish!

Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Cut vegetables and season
  2. Sautee a tsp of oil and place in oven for 30 minutes at 375 degrees
  3. Drizzle greek sauce onto roasted vegetables and serve!
